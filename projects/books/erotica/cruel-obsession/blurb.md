# Amazon Book Description — Cruel Obsession

---

**He doesn't ask. He takes.**

Sofia Marques clawed her way out of poverty to become one of Lisbon's most respected art curators. The Galeria Saudade is everything she's worked for — her sanctuary, her identity, her proof that she is more than the debts her family left behind.

Then she discovers it's about to be destroyed.

The only man who can save it is Aleksandr Volkov — a ruthless billionaire with ties to Lisbon's darkest corners and a reputation that makes powerful men flinch. He owns half the city. And now he wants to own her.

His offer is simple: He'll save Sofia's gallery. But his help comes with conditions.

*Complete access. Complete obedience. Complete surrender.*

Sofia should run. She should say no. She should hate the way his cold eyes strip her bare, the way his voice turns her defiance into something that trembles.

But Aleksandr Volkov doesn't give her a choice. And the most terrifying part?

**She doesn't want one.**

---

*Cruel Obsession is Book 1 of The Obsession Duet, a dark billionaire romance set in Lisbon, Portugal. This book contains an possessive alpha hero, enemies-to-lovers tension, explicit steam, power exchange dynamics, and a cliffhanger ending. Book 2 coming soon.*

**Reader warning:** This is a DARK romance. If you like your heroes polite and your heroines compliant, this isn't for you. If you like obsessive, morally grey men who will burn the world for one woman — welcome home.
