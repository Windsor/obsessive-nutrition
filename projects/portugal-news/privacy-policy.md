# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2 March 2026**

The Portugal Brief ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Portuguese law.

---

## 1. Who We Are

The Portugal Brief is an independent English-language news publication covering Portugal for expats, digital nomads, and anyone interested in life in Portugal.

**Contact:** hello@theportugalbrief.pt
**Website:** https://theportugalbrief.pt

## 2. What Data We Collect

### Newsletter Subscribers
When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:
- Your email address
- The date and time of subscription
- Whether you open our emails or click links within them (engagement data)

This data is collected with your explicit consent and used solely to send you The Portugal Brief newsletter.

### Website Visitors
When you visit our website, we may collect:
- Technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and device type
- Pages visited and time spent on the site
- The website or search engine that referred you

This data is collected via **Cloudflare**, our content delivery and security provider, and used for security, performance monitoring, and aggregate traffic analysis. It is not used to identify you personally.

We do **not** use Google Analytics or other invasive tracking tools. We do not use advertising cookies.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

- **Ghost** (ghost.org) — our publishing platform, which stores subscriber email addresses and manages newsletter delivery preferences.
- **Brevo** (brevo.com) — our email delivery provider, used to send newsletters to subscribers. Brevo processes your email address to deliver emails on our behalf.
- **Cloudflare** (cloudflare.com) — our CDN and security provider, which processes web traffic passing through our domain.

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy policies and GDPR compliance frameworks. We have data processing agreements in place with all providers who handle personal data on our behalf.

---

## 5. How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Subscriber data:** We retain your email address and subscription preferences for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your data is deleted within 30 days.
- **Website traffic data:** Aggregate traffic data is retained for up to 12 months for analysis purposes.

## 6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Right to access** — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification** — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to erasure** — you can ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Right to restriction** — you can ask us to limit how we process your data.
- **Right to data portability** — you can request your data in a portable format.
- **Right to object** — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — you can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email, or by contacting us directly.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **hello@theportugalbrief.pt**. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with Portugal's data protection authority:

**CNPD — Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados**
Rua de São Bento, 148-3º, 1200-821 Lisboa
https://www.cnpd.pt
