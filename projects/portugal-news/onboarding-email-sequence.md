## Implementation Notes

### Ghost Setup (when r2d2 is back)
- Use Ghost's built-in email automation if available, or
- Set up via Brevo (Sendinblue) automation workflows
- Track open rates and click rates for each email
- A/B test subject lines on Email 1 (highest volume)

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate per email (benchmark: 40%+ for welcome series)
- Click rate on guide links
- Reply rate (Email 2 and 4 ask for replies)
- Unsubscribe rate per email (should be <1%)
- Completion rate (% who receive all 5 emails)

### Optimization
- If Email 3 has low opens, test different subject line
- If unsubscribes spike at Email 4, reduce frequency
- Personalize Email 2 guide recommendations based on signup source (if trackable)
