# Cruel Obsession - The Obsession Duet, Book 1

Erotica novella written 2026-02-09. Set in Lisbon. ~20 chapters + epilogue.

**Premise:** Russian-Portuguese billionaire Aleksandr Volkov engineers a crisis at an art gallery to lure curator Sofia Marques into a 30-day arrangement — originally as revenge for her father's betrayal of his family. He falls for her instead. Betrayal revealed, trust shattered, slowly rebuilt.

**Genre:** Dark romance / erotica with literary aspirations
**Setting:** Lisbon — Alfama, Chiado, Bairro Alto, Parque das Nações
**Themes:** Control vs vulnerability, revenge vs love, trust, saudade, art as metaphor

**Key locations:** Galeria Saudade (Chiado), Pensão Amor, Belcanto, MNAA, Feira da Ladra, rooftop on Rua da Atalaia

**Characters:**
- Sofia Marques — art curator, fierce, independent, father was a gambler who fled
- Aleksandr Volkov — billionaire, Russian-Portuguese, mother from Braga, calculated but falling
- Helena Dias — gallery director
- Inês — Sofia's journalist best friend
- Marco Vieira — Aleksandr's right-hand man
- Paulo — driver

**Status:** Book 1 complete. Ends with "To be continued in BEAUTIFUL RUIN (Book 2)"
