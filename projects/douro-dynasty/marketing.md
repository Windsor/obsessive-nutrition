## Amazon Product Description (Blurb)

**He needs a fake fiancée. She needs the money. Neither of them needs to catch feelings.**

Beatriz Castro has clawed her way out of Porto's roughest neighborhood to land her dream job: head enologist at Quinta das Águias, the most prestigious wine estate in the Douro Valley. She's talented, she's hungry, and she is *not* going to let her insufferable new boss ruin this for her.

Tomás Ferreira is the heir to a wine dynasty seven generations deep. He's controlled, intense, and hiding a devastating secret: his dead father's will says he must marry by his next birthday — or lose everything.

When Tomás offers Beatriz a deal — pose as his fiancée for three months in exchange for her mother's medical bills and a guaranteed contract — she knows it's a terrible idea. She says yes anyway.

**The rules are simple:**
Separate bedrooms. Public appearances only. No feelings.

But the Douro Valley in harvest season is forty-five degrees with no air conditioning. The quinta is small. And the traditional grape-treading requires them to march arm-in-arm in the *lagar* while the whole village watches.

Rules were made to be crushed.

**What Beatriz doesn't know:** Tomás chose her for a reason. Her father — the man who walked out when she was six — didn't just leave. He worked for the Ferreiras. And the truth about what happened to him could destroy everything she's building… including whatever is growing between them.

---

**CRUSH is a steamy, enemies-to-lovers dark romance set in the vineyards of Portugal's Douro Valley. It features a possessive hero who falls first, a heroine who takes no prisoners, and enough Portuguese wine and pastéis de nata to make you book a flight. This is Book 1 in the Douro Dynasty series. Each book follows a different couple but shares an interconnected world. HEA guaranteed.**

## Back Cover / Short Blurb (200 words)

She's a working-class wine chemist with something to prove. He's a controlling heir with everything to lose. When Tomás Ferreira offers Beatriz Castro a fake engagement to save his family's empire, she should say no. But her mother needs surgery, and the Douro Valley's most arrogant man is offering to pay.

Three months. Separate bedrooms. No feelings.

Except it's harvest season, the quinta has one working shower, and Tomás Ferreira is not who she thought he was — he's worse. Worse because he's *good*, underneath all that control. Worse because when he looks at her, she forgets every rule she's set.

And worse because he's hiding a secret about her father that could shatter them both.

Set against the sun-scorched vineyards and centuries-old wine cellars of Portugal, *Crush* is a slow-burn dark romance about what happens when two stubborn people pretend not to want each other — and the valley makes liars of them both.
