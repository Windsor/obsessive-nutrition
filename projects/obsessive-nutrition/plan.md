# Obsessive Nutrition — Project Plan

## Concept
Science-first, deep-dive single-food health books for health obsessives.
Series name: **"The Obsessive's Guide to [Food]"** or **"Obsessive Nutrition: [Food]"**

## Revenue Model
1. **KDP royalties** — primary income, 70% royalty on $2.99–$9.99 ebooks
2. **Blog SEO → Amazon sales** — rank for "[food] health benefits" queries, drive book sales
3. **Affiliate links** — supplement/food product recommendations via Amazon Associates
4. **Email list** → future product launches, bundled sets
