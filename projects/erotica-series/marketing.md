## Amazon Product Description (Blurb)

**Behind an unmarked door in Lisbon's oldest neighborhood, there are five floors. Most people never get past the first.**

Vivienne Cross is an expert at uncovering what's been hidden. An American art restorer working in Lisbon's National Tile Museum, she spends her days peeling back centuries of overpainting to reveal the original beauty beneath. Her personal life is another story — a passionless divorce, a career stalled, and a rich fantasy life she's never once acted on.

Then a colleague mentions a place. A palácio in the Alfama district. A society that's existed since 1793, devoted to one thing: **the art of surrender.**

The First Floor is a salon — beautiful, civilized, clothed. The Second Floor is a garden — where doors are open and watching is invited. The Third Floor is a labyrinth — where fantasy becomes flesh.

Vivienne tells herself she'll only stay for a drink. She stays for **Mariana** — a Portuguese photographer with warm hands and an unnerving ability to see exactly what Vivienne has spent her whole life concealing.

Floor by floor, touch by touch, Vivienne discovers that the most terrifying thing in the world isn't being seen naked by strangers.

It's being seen — *truly seen* — by someone who wants you exactly as you are.
