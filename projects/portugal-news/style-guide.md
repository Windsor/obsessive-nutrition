# The Portugal Brief — Style Guide

## Voice & Tone
- **Authoritative but approachable** — we're experts who explain simply
- **Neutral and factual** — we report, we don't editorialize (opinion pieces are clearly labeled)
- **Concise** — respect the reader's time. Lead with the key fact
- **Helpful** — always ask "what does this mean for the reader?"

## Writing Rules

### Headlines
- Lead with the key information
- Use active voice: "Portugal raises minimum wage" not "Minimum wage raised by Portugal"
- Include numbers when possible: "Portugal Golden Visa applications drop 40%"
- No clickbait — deliver on the headline's promise
- Max 80 characters for email subject lines

### Body Copy
- **Inverted pyramid** — most important information first
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use bullet points for lists of 3+ items
- Bold key facts and figures
- Define acronyms on first use: "AIMA (Agency for Integration, Migration and Asylum)"
- Use € for currency (with USD equivalent for major figures)

### Numbers & Data
- Spell out one through nine, use numerals for 10+
- Always use € with a comma for thousands: €500,000
- Percentages: use % symbol, not "percent"
- Dates: "March 1, 2026" in body text, "2026-03-01" in metadata
- Times: use 24h format with timezone: "14:00 WET"

### Portugal-Specific
- Use Portuguese place names with English context: "Braga (northern Portugal)"
- Government bodies: use English name with Portuguese in parentheses on first mention
- Visa types: always capitalize (Golden Visa, D7 Visa, Digital Nomad Visa)
- "Expat" not "expatriate" (our audience prefers the informal)
- "Portugal" not "the Portuguese Republic" (save for formal/legal contexts)

- **[Story 2]** — [1-2 sentence summary]
- [6-8 stories total]

## What to Watch
[1 paragraph on developing stories]

## Markets Snapshot (if Monday)
[Key figures: PSI-20, EUR/USD, property index]

## SEO Guidelines
- Every post needs: title tag, meta description, excerpt, featured image alt text
- Use the primary keyword in: title, first paragraph, one H2, meta description
- Internal link to 2-3 related articles per post
- Use tags consistently (see tag taxonomy below)
- JSON-LD article schema on all posts
- FAQ schema on guides

## Tag Taxonomy
**Topics:** news, politics, economy, immigration, property, tax, healthcare, education, culture, lifestyle, travel, food, weather, sports
**Types:** guide, analysis, opinion, weekly-roundup, market-update, breaking
**Regions:** lisbon, porto, algarve, madeira, azores, alentejo, centro, norte
**Visas:** golden-visa, d7-visa, digital-nomad-visa, student-visa, work-visa

## Image Guidelines
- Featured images: 1200x630px (social sharing optimized)
- Always include alt text describing the image
- Prefer authentic Portugal photography over stock
- Credit photographers when using contributed images
- Logo usage: green (#006400) and red (#FF0000) on white background

## Email-Specific
- Subject line: max 50 characters ideal, 80 max
- Preview text: craft deliberately, don't let it auto-generate
- CTA buttons: one primary CTA per email
- Unsubscribe: always prominent and functional
- Mobile-first: 60%+ of readers on mobile
