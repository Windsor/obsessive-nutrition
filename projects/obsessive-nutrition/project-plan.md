## SEO Strategy
- Target long-tail: "sardines nutrition benefits study", "olive oil polyphenols research"
- Each book = a blog cluster (10-15 supporting articles)
- Comparison posts: "sardines vs salmon omega-3", "extra virgin vs regular olive oil"
- Myth-busting posts rank extremely well: "is [food] actually good for you"
- Internal linking between blog posts and book pages
