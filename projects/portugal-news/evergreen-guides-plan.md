## SEO Strategy
- Each guide: 2000-4000 words
- Target 1 primary keyword + 3-5 secondary keywords
- Include "Last updated: [date]" for freshness signals
- JSON-LD FAQ schema on each guide
- Internal links to related daily news articles
- Meta descriptions optimized for CTR
- Each guide gets its own tag for easy navigation

## Publishing Schedule
- 2-3 guides per week during launch phase
- Update quarterly with fresh data
- Promote via social poster (Bluesky, Reddit when ready)
