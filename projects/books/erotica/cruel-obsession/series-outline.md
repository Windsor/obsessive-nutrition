# The Obsession Duet — Series Outline

## Book 1: Cruel Obsession ✅
Sofia discovers Aleksandr's control over her gallery and her life. Their power struggle escalates into an intense, consuming affair. Ends on a cliffhanger: Sofia discovers Aleksandr orchestrated her family's original financial ruin — he's been watching her for years. She flees into the night, pregnant, though she doesn't know it yet.

## Book 2: Beautiful Ruin
**Word count:** ~22,000
**Timeline:** Picks up 3 weeks after Book 1's cliffhanger.

- Sofia has disappeared. Aleksandr tears Lisbon apart searching for her.
- She's hiding in Porto with her best friend, processing the betrayal.
- She discovers the pregnancy. Conflicted — the child ties her to him forever.
- Aleksandr finds her. But he's different — unraveling without her. We see his full backstory: abusive father, a mother who died because of powerful men, his vow to never be powerless again. Sofia was supposed to be revenge against her father's betrayal of Aleksandr's mother — but he fell in love instead.
- Sofia must decide: Can she love a man who built their relationship on a lie, even if the feelings became real?
- Climax: A rival (Viktor, Aleksandr's half-brother) threatens Sofia to get to Aleksandr. Aleksandr chooses Sofia over his empire.
- Resolution: HEA. They reunite on their terms — equals, not owner and possession. Sofia keeps her gallery, her independence. Aleksandr learns that love isn't control.
- Epilogue: Flash-forward, baby, Lisbon rooftop, full circle.

## Optional Book 3: Dark Sovereign (Spin-off)
**Character:** Marco Vieira — Aleksandr's head of security, introduced in Books 1-2.
- His own dark romance with a journalist investigating Volkov Industries.
- Connects the world, captures readers who want more.
- Can be marketed as "The Obsession Duet World" or start a new series.

## Release Strategy
- Book 1 → Book 2 within 30-45 days (capitalize on cliffhanger urgency)
- Pre-order for Book 2 live at Book 1 launch
- Box set after Book 2 at $4.99-$5.99
- Book 3 spin-off 60 days after box set
